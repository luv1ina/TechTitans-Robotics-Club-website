About Us

We're TechTitans Robotics Club. A FIRST Lego League Team based in Ain Chok Casablanca made of big dreamers. From participating in robotics competitions to hosting coding workshops, our goal is to create opportunities for all students to delve into robotics and learn new skills.

What is FIRST Lego League?

FIRST Lego League (FLL) is a global youth robotics program for students aged 9-16. Teams of students build and program robots using Lego Mindstorms kits to complete challenges based on a theme. The program emphasizes teamwork, innovation, and problem-solving skills.
